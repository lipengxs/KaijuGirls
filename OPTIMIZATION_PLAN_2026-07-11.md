# Kaiju Girls SEO & content plan

## Baseline

GSC: 361 clicks, 6,173 impressions, 5.85% CTR, average position 6.65. The site has a solid multilingual and structured-data base, but `kaiju girls` has 1,809 impressions at 2.71% CTR and position 9.68.

## 0–30 days

- Consolidate release-date, Steam, demo and system-requirements facts into source-linked pages; distinguish confirmed facts from historical plans.
- Remove duplicate canonical and hreflang declarations from templates, retaining one complete reciprocal set per language page.
- Rewrite the homepage snippet around the current player intent: release window, Steam listing, CG/voice scope and guides.

## 31–60 days

- Build distinct character, route/CG tracking and beginner-guide pages, with visible update dates and official source links.
- Localize Japanese and Chinese pages natively instead of translating generic English copy.

## 61–90 days and KPI

- Use a release-monitoring cadence for Steam and developer updates; publish only verified changes.
- Restore overall CTR above 7%, keep the main term in the top six, and grow guide-page clicks to at least 25% of organic clicks.
