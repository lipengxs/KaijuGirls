Kaiju Girls Update Plan and Postponement Announcement

2024-12-06

Hello, dear players who care about our development progress, greetings!

We're here to share the latest updates on Kaiju Girls! Recently, we've been working tirelessly to bring you the most enriching and high-quality gaming experience. Below are some exciting updates to look forward to:

1. More Stunning Artwork & CGs
We aim to deliver a more refined visual experience for players, adding new character illustrations and story CGs. These updates will construct a richer and more vibrant world, making the game's story and visuals even more dynamic.

2. Dynamic Effects and "Director's Cut" Content
By popular demand, we're integrating more dynamic illustrations and CGs to make the visuals truly come alive. Additionally, for the static CG variations drawn by Karasu-chan, we've developed a "Director's Cut" toggle feature. Players will be able to freely switch between these in the game settings to experience Karasu-chan's original vision for Kaiju Girls.

3. Expanded Story Events
To create a deeply immersive story, we're adding more compelling events and branching side plots. Your choices could lead to different endings, offering richer gameplay and narrative depth.

4. Dual-Language Full Voice Acting
That's right! To enhance the storytelling experience, we're preparing full voice acting in both Chinese and Japanese, ensuring the characters express their emotions in the most authentic and heartfelt ways.

5. Original Music Production
To fully realize the game's world-building and deliver the most gripping story, we're committed to crafting original music of the highest quality, ensuring players are fully immersed in the Kaiju Girls universe.

6. Demo Coming Soon
To let everyone get a sneak peek of Kaiju Girls, we're expediting the production of a demo version. This will allow players to experience parts of the game ahead of release.

Once again, we sincerely thank all the players supporting Kaiju Girls. Your patience and anticipation are invaluable to us. Due to the ongoing production of the above updates and after careful consideration, we've decided to postpone the official release date. But don't worry—this delay will absolutely be worth it! We promise to bring you a gaming experience of the highest quality and can't wait to venture into the world of Kaiju Girls with you!
