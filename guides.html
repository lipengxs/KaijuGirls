GAME GUIDES

Your complete guide to Kaiju Girls - Learn about the story, characters, gameplay, and everything you need to know about this immersive visual novel adventure.

Story & World Background

Kaiju Girls is set in a world where years have passed since the battle between the human race and the monsters has ended, and a new era of peace has begun. In the present day, girls who contain the souls of past monsters have appeared and possess the ability to summon their inner monster forms. These special individuals are known as Kaiju Girls (怪獣娘, Kaijū Musume).

An organization called GIRLS has formed to collect and protect these Kaiju Girls. The story of Kaiju Girls focuses on the GIRLS rookie members and their adventures in this new world. Players will experience an emotional journey through a beautifully crafted world, where choices matter and every decision shapes the story's outcome.

The World of Kaiju Girls

The world of Kaiju Girls is a place where the past and present intersect. The remnants of ancient battles between humans and monsters have given way to a peaceful era, but the legacy of those conflicts lives on in the form of Kaiju Girls. These girls carry the spirits of legendary monsters within them, granting them extraordinary abilities and the power to transform into their monster forms.

The GIRLS organization serves as both a sanctuary and training ground for these special individuals. Through the organization, Kaiju Girls learn to control their powers, understand their heritage, and navigate the complexities of a world that has moved beyond the conflicts of the past. The story of Kaiju Girls explores themes of identity, belonging, and the bonds that form between these unique individuals.

Characters

The world of Kaiju Girls is populated by a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique personality, background, and abilities. The main characters are composed of three rookie members of GIRLS, all 16 years old and based on Ultraseven's Capsule Monsters.

Aki Miyashita / Agira

The main viewpoint character of Kaiju Girls, Aki is timid among the trio. She was chosen as the main character due to her interesting situation of being "sandwiched" between energetic and calm characters. Aki is the last to transform into her Kaiju form, which she acquired while saving a kindergartener from a car. Her journey in Kaiju Girls represents growth, courage, and discovering one's inner strength.

Miku Ushimaru / Miclas

The most energetic member of the team in Kaiju Girls, Miku is a fan of Great Monster Fight. She is the first to acquire her Kaiju form due to her admiration for Red King. Miku's enthusiasm and passion drive much of the team's adventures in Kaiju Girls. Her character brings energy and excitement to the story, making her a beloved member of the Kaiju Girls cast.

Reika Shirogane / Windom

A no-nonsense person who, in reality, is a yaoi fangirl. Reika acquired her Kaiju form when reading a yaoi manga. Her character in Kaiju Girls provides a balance between seriousness and humor, creating interesting dynamics within the team. Reika's unique personality adds depth to the Kaiju Girls narrative.

Zetton

A mysterious woman who is actually the champion of Great Monster Fight matches and is GIRLS' strongest cadet. In Kaiju Girls, Zetton views Agira as being similar to her past, creating a mentor-student relationship that adds emotional depth to the story. Her character represents strength, experience, and the wisdom that comes from overcoming challenges.

Pigmon

GIRLS' main idol in Kaiju Girls, who introduced the Soulrizer that allows girls with the spirit of an Ultra Kaiju to transform. Pigmon plays a crucial role in the world of Kaiju Girls, serving as both a guide and inspiration for the rookie members. Her character embodies hope and the potential for transformation.

Red King

One of the strongest fighters in the Great Monster Fight. Despite her strength, Red King also has an affinity for cute things in Kaiju Girls. She usually hunts for Kaiju Girls that went on a rampage from the effects of transforming without the use of Soulrizer. Her character adds complexity to the Kaiju Girls world, showing that strength and gentleness can coexist.

Other Characters

Kaiju Girls features a rich cast of supporting characters including Eleking, Gomora, Zandrias, and others. Each character in Kaiju Girls contributes to the game's rich narrative and world-building, creating a vibrant and engaging experience for players.

Gameplay Guide

Kaiju Girls is an immersive visual novel game that combines stunning artwork with rich storytelling. Here's a comprehensive guide to help you get started and make the most of your experience in Kaiju Girls.

How to Play Kaiju Girls

  • 1. Reading the Story Kaiju Girls follows a visual novel format where you'll read through the story, experiencing beautifully illustrated scenes and character interactions. The narrative in Kaiju Girls unfolds through carefully crafted dialogue and stunning artwork that brings the world to life.
  • 2. Making Choices Throughout Kaiju Girls, you'll encounter decision points that affect the story's progression. Your choices in Kaiju Girls can lead to different story paths, character relationships, and ultimately, different endings. Think carefully about your decisions in Kaiju Girls as they shape your unique experience.
  • 3. Exploring Different Paths Kaiju Girls features multiple branching storylines, encouraging multiple playthroughs. Each path in Kaiju Girls reveals different aspects of the story and character relationships. Replay Kaiju Girls to discover all possible outcomes and endings.
  • 4. Using the Director's Cut Feature Kaiju Girls includes a unique "Director's Cut" toggle function that allows you to switch between different CG variations. Access this feature through the game settings in Kaiju Girls to experience the original vision for certain scenes. This feature in Kaiju Girls enhances replayability and provides additional content.
  • 5. Enjoying Voice Acting Kaiju Girls features full voice acting in both Chinese and Japanese. The professional voice performances in Kaiju Girls bring characters to life with genuine emotions. You can adjust language settings in Kaiju Girls to enjoy the voice acting in your preferred language.
  • 6. Immersing in the Music The original music in Kaiju Girls enhances emotional moments and creates atmosphere throughout the story. The musical score in Kaiju Girls is designed to fully immerse players in the game's world, complementing the narrative and visual elements.

Tips for Playing Kaiju Girls

To get the most out of your Kaiju Girls experience:

  • Take your time reading through the story in Kaiju Girls - the narrative is rich and detailed.
  • Save your game frequently in Kaiju Girls to explore different choice paths.
  • Pay attention to character interactions in Kaiju Girls - they reveal important story elements.
  • Experiment with the Director's Cut feature in Kaiju Girls to see different CG variations.
  • Replay Kaiju Girls multiple times to discover all endings and story paths.
